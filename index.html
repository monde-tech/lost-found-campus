About This System

The Campus Lost and Found System is an official university initiative to help students, faculty, and staff recover lost belongings quickly and efficiently.

🔍

Easy Search

Browse and filter all reported items by category, status, or keyword to quickly find what you're looking for.

📱

Works Everywhere

Access the system from any device — desktop, tablet, or mobile. Share the link with friends to help spread the word.

🔔

Quick Reporting

Submit lost or found reports in minutes with our simple form. Include photos to improve identification accuracy.

🤝

Community Driven

Our system works because of caring campus community members who take time to report what they find.

📊

Live Dashboard

Admins can monitor all reports, update statuses, and track recovery rates in real time from any browser.

🔐

Secure & Private

Contact information is only shared between the item reporter and the admin office to protect everyone's privacy.

Need Help?

Visit the Student Affairs Office at the Administration Building, Ground Floor, or email us at lostfound@campus.edu.ph
